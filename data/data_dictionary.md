# Data Dictionary

**entry_id** – unique identifier  
**timestamp** – ISO datetime of entry  
**category** – somatic, relational, intuition, memory, environment  
**signal_type** – freeze, fawn, flare, tune, hyper-awareness  
**emotion_state** – fear, calm, alert, detached, child-state  
**body_zone** – chest, neck, gut, legs, breath, skin  
**narrative** – detailed contextual description
